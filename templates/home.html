Welcome to WordGuesser!

Are you ready to put your vocabulary to the test and outsmart the clock? WordGuesser is a fun and challenging word-guessing game where you'll need quick thinking, sharp spelling skills, and a bit of luck to come out on top.

How it Works

In WordGuesser, you’ll take on 10 rounds of word-guessing action, where each round gives you a hidden word to uncover, one letter at a time. You can choose from five difficulty levels, ranging from Beginner to Expert, with each level giving you fewer chances to guess the word correctly. But be careful – every wrong guess gets you closer to failure!

Features

  • 5 Difficulty Levels: Beginner (7 guesses), Easy (6 guesses), Medium (5 guesses), Hard (4 guesses), and Expert (3 guesses).
  • Highscore System: Track your best performances, compare your ratio of success and failures, and see how fast you solved the puzzles across different difficulty levels.
  • 10 Rounds per Game: Each game is composed of 10 rounds of exciting word-guessing challenges.
  • Time Pressure: Not only do you need to guess the words, but you also need to do it as quickly as possible to boost your score!

Think You Have What It Takes?

Whether you're playing for fun or trying to beat your personal high score, WordGuesser is the perfect game to keep your mind sharp and your competitive spirit high. Dive into the challenge, improve your guessing game, and see if you can claim the top spot on the leaderboard!
